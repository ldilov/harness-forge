# ADR Template

## Title

Choose a title that states the decision, not the meeting topic.

## Status

- proposed
- accepted
- superseded
- deprecated

## Context

Describe the forces that matter now: scale, reliability, migration cost, team boundaries, deadlines, support window, or compliance needs.

## Decision

State the chosen path directly. Make it possible for a future reader to understand the answer in one paragraph.

## Alternatives Considered

List the strongest alternatives that were genuinely plausible.

## Consequences

- positive effects
- trade-offs and costs
- follow-up actions or guardrails
- what would trigger a revisit
